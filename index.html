    ABOUT STUDIO

    INNOVATION THROUGH COLLABORATION

    TRINETRA GAMES is an independent game development studio founded in 2024, dedicated to creating innovative and immersive gaming experiences. Our team of passionate developers, artists, and designers work collaboratively to push the boundaries of interactive entertainment.

    Based in India, our team of 15-20 talented individuals brings together diverse expertise in game design, programming, art direction, and project management. We believe in creating games that not only entertain but also inspire and challenge players.

    Our mission is to craft extraordinary gaming experiences that resonate with players worldwide. Every project we undertake is approached with meticulous attention to detail and a commitment to excellence that defines our studio culture.
